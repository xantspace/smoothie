What happens after the blend finishes?

The payoff moment — what does the user see?

A

Result Card Slides In

A styled card animates in beside the blender showing the smoothie's name, a color swatch, and the blend's "personality" (e.g., "Tropical Powerhouse"). Includes a Reset button to blend again. Feels like a reveal.

B

Inline Status Update

The blender settles, the status text upgrades to a success message with the blend name and ingredients listed, the button swaps to "Blend Again". Simple, stays in context. (Current approach, improved.)

C

Pour Into a Glass

After blending, an animation shows the smoothie being poured from the jar into a glass that appears beside it. The glass fills with the custom color. Highly theatrical and unique.
